About Sal Hates Waste

We hate waste! And you do too!

Sal helps small businesses and local non-profits connect to donate and/or find surplus food that would otherwise be wasted.

Default to generosity!

All of our services are free. Simply sign-up and post your business' food surplus or search our map for donations.

There are lots of ways to help!

If you would rather give your time, we have some tips on how you can help connect organizations with surplus to groups who could use it.
